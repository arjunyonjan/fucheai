Blackwell AV1 NVENC

RTX 5060 dedicated hardware AV1 encoder. 30-50% smaller than HEVC.

ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -c:v av1_nvenc -preset p6 -cq 23 -rc vbr -b:v 12M -maxrate 16M -b_ref_mode 1 -c:a aac out.mp4
Ampere HEVC b-ref Mode 2

RTX 3060/5060 — hierarchical B-frames for ~15% better compression.

ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -c:v hevc_nvenc -preset p7 -cq 22 -rc vbr -b:v 12M -maxrate 16M -b_ref_mode 2 -c:a aac out.mp4
Blackwell AV1 10-bit (main10)

Blackwell 10-bit encode for HDR content — less banding, better quality.

ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -c:v av1_nvenc -preset p7 -cq 20 -rc:v vbr_2pass -profile:v main10 -pix_fmt p010le -b:v 18M out.mp4
Ampere HEVC 10-bit HDR

HDR encoding with main10 profile — BT.2020, 10-bit depth.

ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -c:v hevc_nvenc -preset p7 -cq 18 -profile:v main10 -pix_fmt p010le -c:a aac out.mp4
All RTX NVENC Lookahead + VBR

Lookahead frame analysis + VBR for smart bitrate distribution across scenes.

ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -c:v hevc_nvenc -preset p7 -cq 20 -rc vbr -b:v 15M -maxrate 20M -bufsize 30M -rc-lookahead 32 -b_ref_mode 2 -c:a aac out.mp4
